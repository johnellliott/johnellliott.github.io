similar.city Blog

Welcome to similar.city. The goal of this site is to help make sense of the world. It allows you to explore a vast amount of data in an interactive map. The data is the result of the analysis and combination of data from academic studies, professional urban rankings, environmental measurements and social media analytics.

This blog goes into detail on some aspects of the site, such as how matches are calculated, what factors affect the matches and analysis of some specific cities. If you just have a quick question, have a look at the FAQ. Or you can go straight to the map to explore the data.

By thinking of cities in comparison to other cities, you can better understand exotic places you have barely heard of. This is thanks to the psychological benefits of using analogues to aud understanding.

If you're from the United States, you probably have a good sense of San Diego's famously perfect climate, but may not know that cities on the north-west of Morocco have very similar climates. Or if you're from Europe you may find the varying cultural matches with former colonies interesting and insightful. Wherever you're from, the economic similarities between the rich world, and the contrast with the developing world is striking.

By changing the sliders in the left hand side, you can choose whether you want to compare cities by their culture, economy or climate, or choose how to combine them into a single score.
